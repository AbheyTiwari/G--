About G-वन

Inspired by the ancient wisdom, "प्रकृति रक्षति रक्षितः" (Nature protects if protected), G-वन is a student-led environmental organization committed to fostering a sustainable future. We believe in active participation and community engagement to make a tangible difference.

Plantation Drives

Organizing regular tree plantation campaigns to increase green cover and combat deforestation.

Waste Management

Promoting recycling, waste reduction, and responsible disposal through awareness programs.

Community Engagement

Engaging local communities and schools in environmental protection initiatives.

Wet Waste Management Project

n a significant stride towards sustainable waste management and environmental stewardship, our society, *G-वन, initiated a **Wet Waste Management Project* in collaboration with the *Indian Pollution Control Association (IPCA). As part of this initiative, **six Aerobins* were installed on our college campus to handle and process wet waste effectively.
